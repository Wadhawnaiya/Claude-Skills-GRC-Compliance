# Security Policy

## Overview

This repository contains compliance guidance and skill definitions for governance, risk, and compliance (GRC) frameworks. While the project itself does not process personal data, this policy records how operators of this repository and its contributors should align with applicable data-protection obligations, notably **India’s Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023 (DPDPA) and DPDP Rules, 2025**.

## Supported Compliance Frameworks

The repository provides skills for 30 frameworks, including:

- **🇮🇳 DPDPA (India)** — Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023; notified rules effective **13 May 2027**.
- GDPR (EU), CCPA/CPRA (California), HIPAA (US), LGPD (Brazil), VN-PDPL (Vietnam), and others.

## Reporting a Vulnerability

If you identify a security issue in this repository (e.g., a guidance error that could lead to non-compliance, a broken link to regulatory text, or a missing notice requirement), please report it responsibly:

1. Email: **security@example.com** (replace with maintainer contact).
2. Do not open a public GitHub issue for security-sensitive findings.
3. Include the skill name, the specific section or rule, and a citation to the correct authoritative source.

## DPDPA India Alignment (Future Maintainers)

Maintainers of this repository distributing the DPDPA skill should:

- Reference only the **Digital Personal Data Protection Act, 2023** and **DPDP Rules, 2025** as notified by the Ministry of Electronics and Information Technology (MeitY).
- Include a clear disclaimer that the skill provides informational guidance, not legal advice.
- Encourage users to consult a qualified Indian data protection lawyer or their Data Protection Officer (DPO) for matters involving significant compliance risk, Data Protection Board proceedings, or complex cross-border scenarios.
- Ensure that any example consent flows, notices, and contractual terms reflect the two-lawful-basis framework (consent under Section 6 and certain legitimate uses under Section 7) and the 18-year threshold for children under Section 9.
- Flag any elements depending on future Central Government notifications (SDF designations, restricted transfer countries, startup exemptions) as provisional.

## Privacy Statement for Contributors

By contributing, you agree that your submission is provided under the repository’s open-source license and that any personal information you include in issues, pull requests, or commits will be processed in accordance with applicable law and the DPDPA (where applicable). Avoid including personal data about third parties in issues or PRs.
